Div And Span Elements

Div Element

The div element is by default a block element, meaning that it takes all available width, and comes with line breaks before and after.

The div element has no required attributes, but style, class and id are common.

England

Oldest Monarchy: England is part of the United Kingdom, which has one of the world's oldest still-functioning hereditary monarchies, dating back over 1,000 years to at least the reign of Alfred the Great in the 9th century.

Birthplace of Shakespeare: England is the birthplace of William Shakespeare, born in Stratford-upon-Avon in 1564, whose works like Hamlet and Romeo and Juliet have profoundly influenced global literature and theater.

Sweden

Fika Culture: Sweden has a beloved tradition called "fika," a daily coffee break where people gather to enjoy coffee, pastries, and conversation, emphasizing relaxation and social bonding.

Right to Roam: Sweden's "Allemansrätten" (Freedom to Roam) allows everyone to freely explore nature, camp, and pick berries or mushrooms on any land, as long as they respect it.

NewZealand

More Sheep Than People: New Zealand has a population of about 5 million people but around 25 million sheep, making it one of the highest sheep-to-human ratios in the world.

Three Official Languages: Alongside English, New Zealand recognizes Māori and New Zealand Sign Language as official languages, reflecting its cultural diversity and heritage.
